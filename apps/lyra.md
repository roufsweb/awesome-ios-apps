## Overview
Lyra is an amazing alternative to premium music streaming services like Spotify or Apple Music. It brings a sleek, highly intuitive interface alongside massive libraries of songs, allowing you to build your ultimate music collection without a subscription.

## Key Features
- **Unlimited Streaming:** Listen to a massive catalog of songs for free.
- **Background Play:** Continuous playback while your screen is locked or while using other apps.
- **Playlist Management:** Create, manage, and curate unlimited playlists.
- **Sleek UI:** A modern, distraction-free player interface.
