## Overview
DNSecure allows you to easily switch your device's DNS resolver. It's a lightweight, privacy-friendly tool that operates natively using iOS's built-in encrypted DNS capabilities.

## Key Features
- **System-Wide Ad Blocking:** By configuring a custom DNS (like AdGuard), you can block ads across browsers and apps without a full VPN.
- **Encrypted DNS:** Supports DNS-over-HTTPS (DoH) and DNS-over-TLS (DoT) for enhanced privacy against your ISP.
- **Lightweight:** Does not drain battery like a traditional VPN app.

## Recommended AdGuard Setup
1. Add a new DNS-over-HTTPS provider.
2. Set Server URL to: `https://dns.adguard-dns.com/dns-query`
3. Activate the profile in your iOS Settings.
