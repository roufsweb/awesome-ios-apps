## Overview
TubeMax is an advanced video player tailored for power users who want maximum control over their video playback. It offers incredible customization options that go beyond the standard YouTube app.

## Key Features
- **Advanced Playback:** Seamless Background Play and PIP modes.
- **Multi-Track Audio:** Easily switch between different language audio tracks when available on a video.
- **Resolution Control:** Force playback at specific resolutions (e.g., 4K or 1080p).
- **Ad-Free Potential:** Enjoy zero interruptions when paired with a custom DNS profile.
