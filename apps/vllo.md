## Overview
VLLO is the perfect intuitive video editor for vloggers and content creators. Unlike many other "free" video editors that force a massive watermark on your exports, VLLO lets you edit and export clean videos for free.

## Key Features
- **Multi-Track Editing:** Manage video, audio, voiceovers, and text on separate timelines.
- **No Watermarks:** Export basic videos cleanly without paying.
- **Easy UI:** Designed for mobile touch interfaces, making trimming and splitting clips a breeze.
- **Royalty-Free Assets:** Includes a selection of free background music, sound effects, and stickers.
