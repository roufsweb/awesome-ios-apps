## Overview
Tube PIP is a powerful, lightweight YouTube alternative that brings premium features to your device completely free of charge. It is perfect for users who want to lock their screen while listening to videos or multitask with a floating window.

## Key Features
- **Background Playback:** Keep listening to videos or music even when you minimize the app or lock your screen.
- **Picture-in-Picture (PIP):** Watch videos in a floating window while using other apps.
- **Clean UI:** A familiar interface that hides Shorts, allowing you to focus purely on long-form content.
- **Ad-Free Potential:** Works seamlessly with AdGuard DNS for a completely ad-free experience.
